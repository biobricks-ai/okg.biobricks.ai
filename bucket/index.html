BioBricks-OKG

An Open Knowledge Graph for Cheminformatics and Chemical Safety

BioBricks-OKG is harmonizing all of the 100+ cheminformatics databases available on biobricks.ai into a single, SPARQL compliant, knowledge graph. It is part of the proto-okn.net project and is available for querying today on frink.
